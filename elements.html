fRUIT CARVINGS

Growing up in Thailand, I was taught to carve fruits and vegetables at school since I was 7 years old. It's a part of our culture that dates back over 700 years. Fruits and vegetables were carved for decorative purposes to enhance the food's appeal way back in the 14th century during the Sukhothai dynasty. As of now, younger Thai generations are not widely practicing this art, so there is a risk of losing this tradition forever. Thus, I will continue carving and will share the skills with people who are interested in learning fruit carving.
